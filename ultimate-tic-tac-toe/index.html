Ultimate Tic Tac Toe

Your turn! Select any cell.

How to Play

1. The game consists of 9 small Tic Tac Toe boards arranged in a 3x3 grid.

2. You play as X, and the computer plays as O.

3. On your turn, you can mark any available cell in any board.

4. Win a small board by getting three of your marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

5. Win the game by winning three small boards in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).
